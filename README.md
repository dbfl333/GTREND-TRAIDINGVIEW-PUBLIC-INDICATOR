# 📈 GTREND (TradingView Indicator)

Welcome to **GTREND** 🚀, an all-in-one custom indicator for TradingView that merges the legendary G Trend strategy with modern Smart Money Concepts (SMC) 🧠, Volume Activity 📊, and Global Session tracking ⏰.

You can use this indicator completely for free on TradingView by adding it to your favorites or copying the open-source script.

### 🛠️ Installation Guide:
1. 📋 Copy the entire code from `gtrend_power_house.pine`.
2. 💻 Open TradingView and navigate to the **Pine Editor** tab at the bottom of your screen.
3. 🧹 Delete any existing code in the editor and paste the copied code.
4. 💾 Click **"Save"** and name it "GTREND".
5. 📊 Click **"Add to Chart"**.

*(Note: This indicator is fully compatible with the new **Pine Script v6** requirements for public publishing. 🌐)*

## 📈 Features Overview

The GTREND is divided into multiple powerful modules, all seamlessly integrated into a single indicator to reduce chart clutter. 🛡️

### 1. ⚡ G Trend Core Strategy
The heart of the indicator. It uses an ATR (Average True Range) trailing stop to identify trend direction.
- **🟢 Buy/🔴 Sell Signals**: Clearly printed labels when the trend shifts.
- **☁️ Dynamic Trend Cloud**: A highlighted colored state filling between the price and the trailing stop line.
- **🎨 Customizable Colors & Transparency**: Fully adjust the Buy/Sell colors and background fill transparency to fit your chart theme perfectly.

### 2. 🧠 Smart Money Concepts (SMC)
Trade like the institutions with built-in structure mapping.
- **🔄 Internal & External Structure**: Automatically labels Change of Character (CHoCH) and Break of Structure (BoS).
- **🧱 Swing Order Blocks**: Identifies high-probability areas of supply and demand based on recent swing highs and lows.
- **⚡ Fair Value Gaps (FVG)**: Highlights imbalances in price action. You can choose to extend violated FVGs or only show the most recent ones.

### 3. 🎯 Auto Fibonacci Retracements
Automatically plots key Fibonacci levels from the most recent major swing points.
- Highlights `0.236`, `0.382`, `0.5`, `0.618`, and `0.786` retracement zones.

### 4. ⏰ Advanced Session Highlights
Visualize the global market sessions directly on your chart backgrounds.
- 🌆 Colors the background during **New York**, **Asia**, and **London** sessions.
- 📊 Displays a real-time data table showing the current session, time until session close, upcoming session, and rolling 4-hour/24-hour volume activity levels.

## ⚙️ Settings Guide

You can tweak the indicator to your exact trading style by clicking the gear icon ⚙️ on the indicator.

- **⚙️ ATR Period & Multiplier**: Adjusts the sensitivity of the G Trend signals. Lower values react faster to price changes. *(Default: 1)*
- **🕯️ Take Wicks Into Account?**: Toggle this to calculate stops based on wicks rather than closing prices.
- **👁️ Fill Transparency**: Dial down the transparency if you prefer subtle background fills for the trend states.
- **🔍 Structure Sensitivity**: Modify how sensitive the script is to identifying major (External) and minor (Internal) structure breaks.
